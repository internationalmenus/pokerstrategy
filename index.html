    Tip Top Flop

    All-In if we have the highest pair on the board.
    We value high pairs the most.


    All-In if we trip on the flop.


    All-In if we pair up & have a real flush draw.
    4 of our suits have hit.


    All-In if we have an open-ended straight draw & have not paired up.


    Cheap real flush chase ok
    Cheap trips chase ok
    Cheap 2-pair chase ok


    Don’t chase backdoors
    Don’t chase straights




    Reminder

    Remember, we often will fold.
    We need our chips to maximize our opportunities when they arrive.




    Tip Top Turn

    Play the player and play the board.
